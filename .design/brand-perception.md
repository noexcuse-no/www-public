# Brand Perception & Tone Guide - No Excuse AS

## Brand Personality
- **Direct:** Say what we mean, no consultant-speak
- **Competent:** Proven results, real experience
- **Scandinavian minimal:** Clean, functional, no fluff
- **Anti-establishment:** Question the obvious, challenge conventions

## Target Perception
- "We say what others think but don't say out loud" - slightly rebellious
- "These guys actually get it done"
- "Not another consultancy with fancy words"
- "Practical, not pretentious"
- "They sound like real people"

## Voice Guidelines

### Do
- Use short sentences
- Be specific and concrete
- Focus on results
- Address problems directly
- Sound like real people talking

### Don't
- Use corporate buzzwords
- Overpromise
- Use jargon
- Be vague
- Sound like a typical consultant

## Language Rules
- **Norwegian Bokmål** - Primary language
- **No consultant-speak** - Avoid: "synergi", "verdiskapning", "bærekraftig" (as buzzwords)
- **Direct** - "Vi hjelper deg" not "Vi tilbyr skreddersydde løsninger"
- **Results-focused** - Focus on outcomes, not process

## Content Examples

### Good
- "Vi finner ut hvor god ledelsen er. På to timer."
- "Snakk med oss uten binding"
- "Vi sier det andre tenker"

### Avoid
- "Vi skaper verdier gjennom strategisk transformasjon"
- "Bærekraftig kompetanseutvikling"
- "Helhetlige løsninger for fremtidens organisasjoner"

## Target Audience Alignment
- People who find lattes pretentious
- Practical, no-nonsense leaders
- SMB founders and managers
- Oslo/Østlandet region
